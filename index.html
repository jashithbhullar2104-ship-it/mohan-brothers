Company Profile

Eight Decades
of Industrial
Heritage

Founded in 1937 in Sakchi, Jamshedpur — the steel city of India — Mohan Brothers has been a cornerstone of the region's heavy industrial ecosystem for over eight decades.

We specialise in mechanical maintenance, precision machining, gear cutting, gearbox services and structural fabrication, serving some of the most demanding plants and facilities in eastern India. Our workshop is equipped with a fleet of heavy-duty lathes (up to 20 feet), boring machines, gear hobbing machines, milling machines, a 100-tonne hydraulic press, and full fabrication tooling — all operated by experienced craftsmen who understand the tolerance and reliability requirements of steel plants, mining operations and heavy manufacturing.

As Jamshedpur's industrial landscape has grown from a single steel mill into a sprawling ecosystem of manufacturing, mining and engineering, Mohan Brothers has grown alongside it — continually investing in capability and building lasting partnerships with clients who rely on us when critical equipment needs to be repaired, modified or manufactured to tight specifications.

Mohan Brothers aims to acquire increasingly complex mechanical fabrication and machining contracts, expand its client base across India's major industrial corridors, and establish itself as one of the leading independent heavy engineering workshops in the country — carrying the legacy of 87 years of honest craftsmanship into India's next industrial era.

CAPABILITIES
Technical Depth

Full-Spectrum
Engineering

A Tata Steel or L&T engineering team needs more than a vendor — they need a workshop partner with the technical breadth to handle whatever the plant throws at them. At Mohan Brothers, decades of hands-on experience across every discipline of mechanical engineering means we understand the engineering context behind every job, not just the machining requirements.

Complex, multi-operation components are handled entirely in-house — from rough casting to final inspection — so clients never have to coordinate across multiple vendors or accept subcontracted quality on critical parts.

Shaft Turning & Grinding

Precision turning of shafts up to 20 feet on our heavy-duty lathe fleet. Stepped shafts, flanged shafts, journal restoration and keyway cutting all performed in-house to close tolerances.

Horizontal Boring

100mm horizontal boring machine for precision bore-sizing of heavy castings, gearbox housings and bearing housings requiring close-tolerance cylindrical bores that cannot be achieved on a lathe.

Gear Hobbing & Profiling

Production of spur and helical gears using our dedicated gear hobbing machine. Replacement gears produced to original specifications from drawings or from worn originals used as pattern.

Surface Planing & Milling

8-foot planer machine and two milling machines for surface finishing, slot cutting, step machining and flat-surface generation on large plate components and structural parts.

Heavy Press Work

100-tonne hydraulic press for bearing fitting and removal, bush pressing, interference-fit assembly, straightening of shafts and structural members, and press-forming operations.

Slotting & Shaping

Dedicated slotter and shaper machines for internal keyway cutting, slot generation, spline profiles and intricate internal contour work not achievable on a lathe or milling machine alone.

Reverse Engineering

When OEM spares are unavailable or lead times are unacceptable, we reverse-engineer worn or damaged components from physical measurement — producing functional replacements that meet or exceed original specifications.

Structural Fabrication

Custom steel structures, machine bases, conveyor frames, equipment guards and support structures fabricated to client drawings or developed collaboratively with your engineering team.

Gearbox Overhaul & Assembly

Complete strip-down, dimensional inspection, component replacement — gears, bearings, seals, bushings — and precision reassembly to OEM clearances. Trial-run testing before return to plant.
